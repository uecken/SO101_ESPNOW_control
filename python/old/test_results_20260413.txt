Notes:

    This is servo response delay, not ESP-NOW latency.
  - Gripper (ID=6) showed near-perfect tracking (avg 1.4 steps = 0.12 degrees)
    because it was stationary during test.
  - 1 step = 360/4096 = 0.088 degrees
  - Average error across all axes: 43.8 steps = 3.9 degrees

Previous Issues (resolved):
  - ID=5 (wrist_roll) and ID=6 (gripper) were not moving on Follower.
    Root cause: Min_Position_Limit and Max_Position_Limit were set to
    extremely narrow ranges (7-10 steps) from a prior incomplete
    LeRobot calibration. Fixed by running full calibration with
    so101_calibrate.py --port COM24.
  - ID=1 (shoulder_pan) had limited tracking range (296 steps).
    Also fixed by recalibration.
